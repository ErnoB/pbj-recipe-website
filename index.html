British Breakfast Recipe!

Learn how to make a delicouse English Breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Tin of beans

  • butter
  • some bacon
  • Mushrooms
  • sausages
  • 2-3 eggs
  • Tomatoes

Instructions

  1. First you have to chop the mushrooms and the tomatoes.
  2. Then you have to get a pan and warm it up just so that you can cook the eggs and bacon
  3. when the pan is warmed up, you can cook the eggs and bacon on the pan
  4. With a another pan, you can open the tin of beans and warm it up using the pan or just microwave it.
  5. using the same pans that you cooked the eggs and bacon, you can throw in the mushrooms and tmatoes in the pan for 2-3 minuits
  6. with the bread you can toast it and spread some butter on it
  7. Put all your food onto a plate and then your done
